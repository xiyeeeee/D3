Sarawak Visitor Arrivals





In the three years span of 2017-2019, while foreign tourist numbers experienced a slight decline, domestic tourism witnessed a discernible upward trajectory. A noteworthy overall large scale decline was witnessed in year 2020 where both domestic and foreign tourist arrivals undergone a significant drop. This is likely influenced by the global impact of COVID-19, with total arrivals plummeting to 1,199,872. Foreign and domestic tourist numbers dropped to 363,304 and 836,568, respectively. The repercussions of the pandemic persisted in 2021, reflected in a total of 212,867 arrivals, dominated by 235,245 foreign tourists and a considerably lower 22,378 domestic tourists.
